BSc Nursing

About the Course, Syllabus, Affiliation

Unlock a path to excellence with Kaveri Institutions, offering the best B.Sc nursing admission in Bangalore. Among the top nursing colleges in Bangalore, we prioritize quality education, state-of-the-art facilities, and hands-on training. Join us to experience a nurturing environment that shapes competent and compassionate nursing professionals. As one of the best nursing colleges, we are dedicated to providing holistic education and fostering future leaders in healthcare.

The students of nursing are trained to take care of the individuals, families, communities around them and help them attain a healthy living. They are also trained on various aspects such as, team management, decision making, interpersonal skills etc.

The course gives in-depth knowledge in the medical-related subjects like Anatomy, Physiology, Nutrition, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Ophthalmology, Nephrology etc.

The Kaveri Institutions is one of the best B.Sc Nursing colleges in Bangalore for quality nursing training courses.

DURATION
4 Years (8 SEMESTERS)
ELIGIBILITY
Pass in PUC, +2 or equivalent

Eligibility for B.Sc. Nursing

The students who are looking for best B.Sc Nursing Admission in Bangalore can contact Kaveri Institutions. The students who have completed their PUC or 10+2 course are eligible to apply for B.Sc Nursing course at Kaveri Institutions.

The students should compulsory appear for Entrance Exam conducted by KEA (Karnataka Examinations Authority) and get qualified as per government order.

Duration of Course

The B.Sc. Nursing course in Bangalore at Kaveri Institutions is a 4-year degree programme that is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS).
